OUR ORIGINS.

"Impractical Creations was founded in 2018 by Jesus Almaraz Argueta. While interested in academia, Jesus also harbored a deep interest in video games. More specifially, he became interested in design theory and the concept of games as art. This interest motivated him create games as means of exploring ideas.
