OUR ORIGINS.

"Impractical Creations was founded in 2018 by Jesus Almaraz Argueta. While interested in academia, Jesus also harbored a deep interest in video games. More specifically, he became interested in design theory and the concept of games as art. This interest motivated him to create games as a means of exploring ideas. This page contains some more information about him."

PROFILE

SUMMARY.

"Jesus Almaraz Argueta is a rising first-year mathematics student at the University of Chicago. He is originally from Guanajuato, Mexico. However, he has spent the latter half of his life in Clewiston, Florida. Since a young age, Jesus displayed a strong inclination toward academia. Nonetheless, this has not restricted his exploration of other interests. Impractical Creations emerged as an exploration of these interests. While the main medium of Impractical Creations is video games, these projects incorporate ideas and concepts from other areas of interest."
